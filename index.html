Answers to the most frequent questions

What is RetoSwap, and how does it work?
RetoSwap is a peer-to-peer trading network, not a website or centralized service. It's software you run on your own hardware, which connects to other people running the RetoSwap software to facilitate trades.
Is RetoSwap safe to use?
RetoSwap is open-source and community-driven with a focus on privacy realized through the use of End-to-End encryption, a decentralized network, Tor and Monero to ensure your personal information and transaction details remain secure.
Which currencies does RetoSwap support?
RetoSwap supports most fiat currencies through a big variety of payment methods:
From regular SEPA / ACH transfers over apps like PayPal & CashApp to physical cash sent via mail or even in-person.

The following cryptocurrencies are supported as well:
Monero (XMR), USDT (on ETH & TRON), Ethereum (ETH), Bitcoin (BTC), Litecoin (LTC) & Bitcoin Cash (BCH).
Additional currencies and payment methods may be added as the platform evolves.
What are the requirements to use RetoSwap?
Currently you need a minimum of 0.11 XMR to fund security deposits. There are ongoing efforts to drop this requirement in the future.

You will also have to create a profile (called trade account, data is stored locally and only shared with your trade partner) within RetoSwap before you are able to make your first trade.
Do I need to verify my identity to use RetoSwap?
No, RetoSwap itself is merely open source software, without the ability to verify user identities. Users are required to do their own research and adhere to the laws of their local jurisdictions & regulations regarding cryptocurrency transactions.
How are disputes or trade conflicts resolved?
RetoSwap has an integrated arbitration system. If a conflict arises, an arbitrator reviews the evidence provided by both parties and resolves the dispute fairly and transparently.
What fees does RetoSwap charge?
RetoSwap would usually charge a small fee to support platform development and maintenance but currently fees are 0% (zero) until at least 2025-12-31. Future changes to the fee structure will be communicated well in advance.
How long does a typical transaction take?
Transaction times depend on the payment method and the availability of your trade partner. Cryptocurrency transactions typically complete within minutes, while fiat transfers may take up to a week depending on the payment method (e.g., SEPA, ACH).
Can I use RetoSwap in my country?
RetoSwap is accessible worldwide. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with the laws of their local jurisdiction.
